A Question of Balance

A thought-provoking murder mystery probing the uneasy space where psychology, philosophy, and religion collide.

A novel by David S. Sherman

For Dr. Steven Gold, a single patient may unearth the truth behind a murder—and himself.

A Question of Balance is a cerebral and emotionally charged mystery that explores the fragile terrain of guilt, belief, and the cost of self-knowledge.

As a psychologist’s search for truth deepens, questions of meaning and identity begin to blur the boundary between patient and self.

Dreams in Amber

Author

David S. Sherman, Ph.D., is a licensed psychotherapist and serial entrepreneur whose work explores identity, guilt, and belief.

His fiction explores the uneasy intersection of psychology, philosophy, and the limits of truth.
